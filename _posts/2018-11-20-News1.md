title: I got second prize in undergraduate competition in 2018 KIIE!
---

I participated in the competition for undergraduate student projects in KIIE and got second prize!  

KIIE stands for Korean Institute of Industrial Engineers, which is my major's institute.  
My subject was warning system for swells using time series analysis and deep learning.  
I not only used ARIMA, ARMA, windowed MLP, and LSTMs to classify and generate weather data, also tried reinforcement learning.  

The performance was not that exellent, but we tried to solve some challenging proplems.  
I think that's why we got the second prize.
